American Murder: The Family Next Door

Genre: True Crime • Length: 1h 23m • Year: 2020 • Where to Watch: Netflix

Using raw, firsthand footage, this documentary examines the disappearence of Shannan Watts and her children, and the terrible events that followed. The film tells the story of the 2018 Watts family murders, which took place in Frederick, Colorado. It uses archival footage including social media posts, law enforcement recordings, text messages and home video footage to depict the events that occurred.
