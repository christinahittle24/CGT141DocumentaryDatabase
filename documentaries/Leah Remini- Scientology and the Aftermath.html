Leah Remini: Scientology and the Aftermath

Genre: Religion • Length: series • Year: 2016 • Where to Watch: Hulu

Leah Remini: Scientology and the Aftermath is a documentary series that explores the controversial Church of Scientology and its practices. Through interviews with former members and experts, the series sheds light on the alleged abuses and manipulations within the organization.
