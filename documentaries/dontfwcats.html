Don't F**k With Cats: Hunting an Internet Killer

Genre: True Crime • Length: Series • Year: 2019 • Where to Watch: Netflix

A twisted criminal's gruesome videos drive a group of amateur online sleuths to launch a risky manhunt that brings them into a dark underworld. The series chronicles events following a crowd-sourced amateur investigation into a series of animal cruelty acts committed by Canadian pornographic actor Luka Magnotta, culminating in his murder of Jun Lin, a student from China who was studying at Concordia University.
