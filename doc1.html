Heaven's Gate: The Cult of Cults

Genre: Religion • Length: 204 min • Year: 2020

Heaven's Gate: The Cult of Cults is a four-part Max Original docuseries that provides a thorough, in-depth examination of the infamous UFO religious cult, from its origins in the 1970s to its tragic conclusion in 1997. The series explores the stranger-than-fiction circumstances that culminated in the largest mass suicide on U.S. soil.
